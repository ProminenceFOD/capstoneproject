## Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

### Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

### Our Standards
We expect all participants to:
* Be respectful and considerate.
* Use inclusive language.
* Refrain from harassing behavior.
* Respect differences of opinion.

### Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

For a full version, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
